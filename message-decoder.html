📖 Cipher Guide

Caesar Cipher: Shifts each letter by a fixed number. A→D with shift 3.

Reverse: Reverses the entire message. "Hello" → "olleH"

Pigpen Style: Replaces letters with symbols.

Binary: Converts text to binary code (0s and 1s).

Morse Code: Uses dots and dashes.

Atbash: Reverses the alphabet. A→Z, B→Y, C→X.
